Research Papers

A curated collection of my recent research contributions and technical whitepapers in the fields of Artificial Intelligence and Data Engineering.

1. Controlling Foundation Models via Internal Interventions: A Survey of Inference-Time Methods

Authors: Varnith Chordia, Pradeep Bolleddu, Abhishek Pandir, Karn Tiwari

A comprehensive survey exploring how internal interventions during inference can be used to steer and control foundation models effectively.

This survey provides a detailed overview of inference-time methods for controlling foundation models. We analyze various internal intervention strategies, discussing their theoretical foundations, practical implementations, and the trade-offs involved in steering model behavior without retraining.

2. Multimodal GraphRAG with Cross-Modal Alignment: Towards Unified Video Understanding and Knowledge Graph Construction

Authors: Pradeep Bolleddu, Anirudh, Hrishikesh Teldelkar

Developing a unified framework for video comprehension through multimodal knowledge graph extraction and cross-modal semantic alignment.

This research introduces Multimodal GraphRAG, a system designed to improve video understanding by aligning visual features with structured knowledge. We propose a pipeline for constructing dynamic knowledge graphs from video streams and utilizing cross-modal alignment to facilitate more accurate retrieval and reasoning.

3. How a SAPIEN Is Born: A Journey to Discover Their Tribe and Latent Traits for Human Behavior Simulation

Authors: Taranveersingh, Aanatraj, Samanvitha, Harvinder, Pradeep Bolleddu

Investigating the emergence of latent traits and tribal identities in digital entities for advanced human behavior simulation.

This work presents a framework for human behavior simulation focusing on 'SAPIEN' entities. We explore the discovery of latent traits and the formation of 'tribes' within simulations to create more realistic and nuanced social dynamics.

4. CardioLMBench: A GPU Performance Benchmark for Small Language Models in Cardiovascular Medicine

Authors: Pradeep Bolleddu, Madhurima Vardhan, Sanjai Balasubramainam

A performance benchmark focused on the efficiency of small language models (SLMs) running on GPUs within the context of clinical cardiovascular data.

CardioLMBench addresses the need for domain-specific benchmarking for SLMs. We evaluate GPU performance and inference latency for models specialized in cardiovascular medical terminology, providing a baseline for clinical deployment.

5. See Your Medicine Work: Personalized 3D Pharmacological Visualizations

Authors: Pradeep Bolleddu, Lekshmi, Spruthi Moore

Using 3D graphics and patient-specific data to visualize pharmaceutical interactions within the human body.

This project bridges the gap between complex pharmacology and patient understanding through personalized 3D visualizations. We detail a pipeline that transforms medical data into interactive simulations of drug efficacy.

6. Compositional Safety Analysis via Interaction Risk Graphs: A Graph Neural Network Framework for Occupational Hazard Prediction

Authors: Pradeep Bolleddu, Dr. Sushmita, Shanu

Applying Graph Neural Networks (GNNs) to model and predict complex risks in workplace environments through interaction analysis.

We propose a GNN-based framework for occupational safety. By constructing interaction risk graphs, our model identifies potential hazards by analyzing the compositional relationships between workplace variables and human factors.
