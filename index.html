I’m Pradeep Bolleddu, an Applied Scientist at a stealth AI startup in Seattle, Washington. My research and engineering work spans computer vision, LLM performance modeling, and building scalable ML systems- I have a Strong research background in computer vision and image processing, including multi-cameras, Multiviews 3D computer vision, content-based image/video retrieval, mobile augmented reality, image synthesis and object segmentation.

I joined UMass Chan Medical School in November 2023 as a Machine Learning Researcher, building cloud-native DICOM ETL and preprocessing pipelines on Google Cloud Dataflow for brain MRI. Implemented CNNs and generative models (VAEs/GANs) for representation learning and augmentation. Following that, I completed a three-month internship at Third AI Automation, where I implemented Novel LLM Foundational Models and RAG systems for Southern California Edison.

Previously, I was a MSDS Graduate Student at the University of Massachusetts, where my curiosity drew me to generative-AI applications in healthcare under Prof. Julia Fang. With Julia, I focused on translating generative models into privacy-conscious, clinically relevant workflows that bridge research and practice. After 2nd Semester, partnered with Prof. Scott Field to scale experiments on high-performance computing (OpenMCP), learning to adapt models and pipelines for distributed training and large datasets at CSCDR Lab. It was wonderful collabrating with Prof. Vijaya Chalivendra on several campus projects.

Before joining UMass, I've completed my bachelor’s degree at Indian Institute of technology- IIT Ropar, During 6th semester onwards I have worked with Dr. Ekta Singla on an Autonomous WardBot. I’m passionate about blending research-grade models with production systems - from medical imaging and prognosis models to LLM-powered retrieval systems. I enjoy open-source work, designing experiments, and shipping solutions that make measurable impact.
